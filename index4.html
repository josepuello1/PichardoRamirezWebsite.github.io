About Us

Founded in 1973, Pichardo, Ramirez & Co., SRL has been a cornerstone of industrial trade in the Dominican Republic. We serve as trusted representatives for global manufacturers and suppliers, facilitating crucial connections between international producers and local industry.

Why Choose Us

  • Deep understanding of local business culture
  • Extensive global network of suppliers
  • Comprehensive support including logistics and payment processing
  • Multi-generational commitment to excellence
