About Us

Since 1973, Pichardo, Ramirez & Co., SRL has been a trusted leader in industrial trade within the Dominican Republic. We specialize in bridging the gap between world-class manufacturers and local industries, acting as the reliable representative and strategic partner for global suppliers.

Our commitment is to deliver exceptional service and quality products combined with competitive pricing, ensuring our clients consistently choose us as their preferred partner for sourcing industrial materials. With decades of experience and deep market insight, we drive value and growth for both manufacturers and businesses across the region.

Why Choose Us

  • 50+ years of market expertise
  • Deep understanding of global and local business culture
  • Committed to securing mutually profitable outcomes for all parties involved
  • Extensive network of global suppliers and local customers
  • Comprehensive support including industry knowledge, leads, sales, sourcing, logistics and payment processing
  • Multi-generational commitment to excellence
